Create a User at any role such as your own or lower. A password will be generated and an email will be sent to the address specified. You may also specify an expiry for Poll Admins as well as a location.

You may edit a user by specifying their username and modifying their details as long as the account is below you.

You may delete a user by specifying their username as long as the account is below you.
